★★ 进阶级 · 句型理解

阅读改写段落，选择正确答案

1. 工效学风险认知
In our workplace, it is unrealistic to expect zero ergonomic risk. Certain positions still face typical hazards such as repetitive motion and excessive force.
Q: According to the passage, which statement is TRUE?
A) All positions have achieved zero ergonomic risk.
B) Some positions still have ergonomic hazards.
C) Repetitive motion is not an ergonomic hazard.
D) Excessive force only happens in offices.
显示答案
答案：B — 原文明确说"部分岗位仍存在典型工效危害"
2. MSDs成因与分类
When employees experience long-term micro-traumas from poor workplace design, these can develop into Work-related MSDs, also called cumulative trauma disorders (CTDs) in ergonomics.
Q: What does "cumulative" mean in CTDs?
A) Happening suddenly at one time
B) Building up gradually over time
C) Affecting only one body part
D) Caused by a single accident
显示答案
答案：B — cumulative意为"累积的"，指伤害随时间逐步累积
3. 管控层级与措施
The company implements full-cycle controls including engineering modification, job rotation, ergonomics training and PPE. Among these, engineering modification targets the hazard source, while job rotation reduces individual exposure time.
Q: What is the main difference between engineering modification and job rotation?
A) Engineering is cheaper than job rotation
B) Engineering targets the source; job rotation limits exposure time
C) Job rotation eliminates the hazard completely
D) They are the same thing with different names
显示答案
答案：B — 工程改造针对危害源头，岗位轮换减少个人暴露时长
★★★ 挑战级 · 场景迁移

将所学表达迁移到新场景，用英文回答

场景1：办公室工效学
You are an EHS officer in a tech company. Several employees complain about wrist pain and back ache after long hours at their desks. Use at least 3 key expressions from this lesson to write a short recommendation (3-4 sentences).
提示关键词：repetitive motion / awkward posture / engineering modification / job rotation
参考答案
Their wrist pain is likely caused by repetitive motion from continuous typing, and back ache may result from awkward posture at poorly adjusted desks. I recommend engineering modification such as providing adjustable chairs and monitor stands, along with job rotation to alternate desk work with other tasks.
场景2：车间风险评估汇报
You need to report to the GM about ergonomic risks in the assembly line. Write a 2-sentence summary covering: 1) what the hazards are, 2) what controls you recommend.
提示关键词：ergonomic hazard / excessive force / MSDs / full-cycle controls
参考答案
The assembly line has ergonomic hazards including excessive force from heavy lifting, which may lead to Work-related MSDs. We should implement full-cycle controls including mechanical assists and job rotation to continually reduce risk.
场景3：新员工培训
A new colleague asks: "I've heard of CTDs but don't understand them. Can you explain in simple English?" Write a 2-3 sentence explanation.
提示关键词：cumulative trauma disorder / micro-traumas / ergonomic hazard
参考答案
CTDs stand for cumulative trauma disorders. They happen when small injuries — called micro-traumas — build up over time because of ergonomic hazards like repetitive movements or poor posture. If we fix the workplace design early, we can prevent these injuries before they become serious.
