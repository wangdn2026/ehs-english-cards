10分钟对话练习：人体功效学

Part 1: ★ 识别危害（2分钟）
A: Does our factory achieve zero ergonomic risk for all working positions?
A: 我们工厂所有岗位是否已经实现人体工效学零风险？
B: We cannot achieve absolute zero ergonomic risk. Some operational positions still have typical ergonomic hazards.
B: 我们无法实现绝对的工效零风险，部分作业岗位仍然存在典型的人体工效学危害。
A: What kind of ergonomic risks exist in our workplace?
A: 我们现场主要存在哪些工效学风险？
B: We have five main ergonomic hazard factors: repetitive motion, excessive force, awkward static postures, contact stress and hand-tool vibration.
B: 现场主要存在五大工效风险因子：重复性动作、过度施力、不良静态体态、接触压迫应力以及手持设备振动。
A: Who is most at risk?
A: 哪些岗位接触风险最大？
B: Assembly line workers, maintenance staff, and warehouse handlers face the highest ergonomic risk.
B: 流水线操作工、维修人员和仓库搬运工面临的工效风险最大。
Part 2: ★★ 健康后果（2分钟）
A: What potential injuries will these ergonomic hazards bring to our employees?
A: 这些工效危害，会给员工带来哪些职业伤害？
B: These long-term accumulated micro-traumas will cause Work-related MSDs, also known as cumulative trauma disorders in ergonomics.
B: 这类长期累积的微小创伤，会引发工作相关肌肉骨骼疾患，也就是工效学中的累积性创伤障碍。
A: What does MSDs stand for exactly?
A: MSDs具体指什么？
B: MSDs stands for Musculoskeletal Disorders. It covers injuries to muscles, tendons, nerves, and joints caused by workplace factors.
B: MSDs是肌肉骨骼疾患的缩写，涵盖因工作因素导致的肌肉、肌腱、神经和关节损伤。
A: Are these injuries reversible?
A: 这些损伤可逆吗？
B: Most early-stage MSDs are reversible with proper intervention, but chronic cases may become permanent disabilities.
B: 早期MSDs经过适当干预多数可逆，但慢性病例可能发展为永久性功能丧失。
Part 3: ★★ 风险评估方法（2分钟）
A: Do we have a procedure for ergonomic risk management?
A: 针对工效学风险，我们是否建立了管控程序？
B: Yes, we have a complete ergonomic risk control procedure based on OSHA standards.
B: 有的，我们依据OSHA标准，建立了完整的工效风险管控程序。
A: What methods do you use to identify ergonomic hazards and assess risks on site?
A: 你们通过哪些方式，对现场工效风险进行识别和评估？
B: We confirm job tasks, identify risk factors, and complete professional evaluation with standardized ergonomics checklists and assessment tools.
B: 我们先确认岗位作业内容、识别风险因子，再使用标准化工效核查清单和专业工具完成风险评估。
A: How often do we conduct these assessments?
A: 多久做一次评估？
B: We perform full assessments annually and targeted reviews whenever there are process or equipment changes.
B: 每年做一次全面评估，工艺或设备变更时随时追加专项评估。
Part 4: ★★★ 管控措施（2分钟）
A: After risk assessment, what systematic control measures have we implemented?
A: 风险评估完成后，我们落地了哪些系统化的管控措施？
B: We adopt full-cycle controls including engineering modification, job rotation management, ergonomics training and necessary PPE protection to reduce MSDs risks continually.
B: 我们执行全周期管控措施，包含工程改造、岗位轮换管理、工效安全培训、按需配备个人防护用品，持续降低肌肉骨骼疾患风险。
A: What does engineering modification include specifically?
A: 工程改造具体包括什么？
B: It covers adjustable workstations, mechanical lifting aids, anti-fatigue mats, and tool redesign to reduce force and repetition.
B: 包括可调节工作台、机械升降辅助设备、防疲劳地垫，以及降低施力和重复度的工具改造。
A: How does job rotation help?
A: 岗位轮换有什么作用？
B: By rotating workers through different tasks, we limit their exposure time to specific ergonomic risk factors and allow muscle groups to recover.
B: 通过轮换不同岗位，限制员工对特定工效风险因子的接触时长，让肌群得到恢复。
Part 5: ★★★ 完整讨论（2分钟）
A: What's the most challenging part of ergonomic risk management?
A: 工效风险管理中最有挑战性的部分是什么？
B: Getting workers to maintain proper posture and use ergonomic equipment correctly. Many revert to old habits when unsupervised.
B: 最难的是让员工保持正确体态、正确使用工效设备。很多人无人监督时又回到老习惯。
A: How do we address this?
A: 这个问题怎么解决？
B: Through regular ergonomics training and buddy systems. We also post visual reminders at workstations showing correct postures.
B: 通过常态化工效培训和伙伴互助机制，在工位张贴正确体态的视觉提示。
A: How do we monitor effectiveness?
A: 如何监测管控效果？
B: We track MSDs incidence rates quarterly and conduct follow-up risk assessments after each engineering modification.
B: 每季度追踪MSDs发病率，每次工程改造后做后续风险评估。
A: Any continuous improvement plans?
A: 有持续改进计划吗？
B: Yes, we plan to introduce wearable posture monitors, upgrade all standing desks, and expand job rotation to more departments.
B: 有。计划引入可穿戴体态监测设备、全面升级站立式办公桌，并把岗位轮换推广到更多部门。
A: Great. Ergonomic management is a long-term commitment.
A: 很好，工效管理不是短期任务，需要长期坚持。
B: Exactly. With proper controls and worker engagement, we can protect everyone's musculoskeletal health for a lifetime.
B: 没错，只要管控到位、员工自觉配合，就能保障每一位工人的肌肉骨骼健康。
