1
噪声辨识与监测Noise Identification & Monitoring
GM 总经理
How do we implement an overall occupational Noise Exposure Control Program in our factory currently?
我们目前该如何在本厂落地一套完整的职业噪声暴露管控方案？
EHS Manager 安全主管
We conduct noise hazard identification and risk assessment every year, file all high-noise equipment such as air compressors, grinders and stamping machines, monitor noise levels annually for all noise-exposed posts and semi-annually for high-noise posts, and complete risk classification assessment.
我们每年全域排查噪声源，空压机、打磨机和冲压机等高噪设备全部建档，每年噪声岗位监测一次，高噪岗位半年监测一回，做完风险分级评估。
noise hazard identification噪声危害辨识 risk assessment风险评估 noise monitoring噪声监测
2
风险分级Risk Classification
GM 总经理
What standards do we adopt for risk ranking? And what corresponding measures will be taken for each ranking?
是依据什么标准划分风险等级，不同等级分别采取什么相应的举措？
EHS Manager 安全主管
Four risk levels are ranked by 8h A-weighted equivalent sound level.
以8小时等效A声级划分四档风险。
risk ranking风险分级 8h A-weighted8小时等效A声级
3
管控层级Hierarchy of Controls
GM 总经理
What priority hierarchy do we follow for noise control, and what solutions are prioritized?
噪声治理遵循什么管控顺序，优先用哪些治理手段？
EHS Manager 安全主管
We follow the priority: Elimination > Substitution > Engineering Control (Source Control > Transmission Path Control) > Administrative Control > PPE. We prioritize purchasing low-noise equipment and installing vibration damping and sound absorption facilities.
遵从消除>替代>工程控制(源头控制>传播路径)>管理手段>个体防护的优先级，优先采购低噪设备、加装减震消音设施这类工程改造方式。
hierarchy of controls管控层级 engineering control工程控制 hearing conservation program听力保护计划
4
管理控制与个体防护Administrative Control & PPE
GM 总经理
What specific measures have been developed from administrative control and employees' personal protection perspective?
管理层面和员工个人防护方面采取了哪些具体措施？
EHS Manager 安全主管
In the high-noise areas, implement job rotation & working hour limits. Earplugs or earmuffs are mandatory for posts over 85 dB(A), with regular fit-test and unified replacement of protective equipment.
划定高噪区域、执行轮岗限制作业时长；85dB(A)以上岗位强制佩戴耳塞耳罩，定期做防护用品密合度测试与统一更换。
administrative control管理控制 PPE fit-test密合度测试 job rotation轮岗
5
职业健康监护Health Surveillance
GM 总经理
How do we conduct occupational health surveillance for frontline staff working in high-noise-exposed posts?
一线高噪岗位员工的职业健康监护工作如何推进？
EHS Manager 安全主管
All employees receive pre-employment, routine and exit audiometric tests with independent health files. Staff with hearing abnormalities are transferred promptly, and noise protection training is conducted regularly.
全员落实上岗、在岗、离岗三次听力检测，一人一档建立健康台账，听力异常人员及时调岗，常态化开展噪声防护培训。
occupational health surveillance职业健康监护 audiometric test听力检测
6
档案管理Records Management
GM 总经理
How do we manage daily inspection and relevant documents & records?
日常检查和相关资料档案是如何管理的？
EHS Manager 安全主管
Monthly on-site walkthrough and annual procedure revision are conducted with closed-loop hazard control mechanism. Noise logs, monitoring records, medical exam records, and training records are uniformly archived, with retention period no less than 30 years.
每月现场巡查，每年修订管控程序，建立隐患闭环整改；噪声台账、监测、体检、培训记录统一归档，留存年限不少于30年。
records management档案管理 closed-loop闭环
7
变更管理Management of Change
GM 总经理
Are there supplementary noise control procedures after new processes or new equipment are put into use?
新工艺、新增设备进场后，噪声管控有没有追加流程？
EHS Manager 安全主管
Additional noise hazard identification, monitoring and control will be launched immediately after process or equipment changes, re-assess risk levels, and update control plans to eliminate or control noise hazards.
一旦工艺、设备变更，立刻追加噪声辨识、监测和控制，重新评估风险等级，同步更新管控方案，消除或控制噪声隐患。
management of change变更管理
