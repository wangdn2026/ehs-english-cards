10分钟对话练习：噪声危害管理
← 返回星级目录  |  显示全部
Part 1: ★ 识别危害（2分钟）
A: Do you know what occupational noise is?
A: 你知道什么是职业噪声吗？
B: Yes, it refers to unwanted sound in the workplace that may cause hearing damage over time.
B: 知道，它是指工作场所中长时间接触可能损害听力的非必要声音。
A: Which areas in our factory are noisy?
A: 我们工厂哪些区域噪声比较大？
B: The production workshop, compressor room, and cutting area are the main sources.
B: 生产车间、空压机房和切割区域是主要的噪声来源。
A: Who is most at risk?
A: 哪些岗位接触风险最大？
B: Machine operators, maintenance workers, and forklift drivers are at highest risk.
B: 设备操作人员、维修人员和叉车司机面临的危害风险最大。
Part 2: ★★ 噪声源与标准（2分钟）
A: Can you name the main noise sources?
A: 你能说出主要的噪声源有哪些吗？
B: Sure. Punch presses, air compressors, grinding machines, and ventilation fans are the biggest sources.
B: 当然。冲压机、空压机、打磨机和通风风机是最大的噪声源。
A: What's the decibel level in these areas?
A: 这些区域的分贝值大概在什么范围？
B: The punch press area can reach 95 to 105 dB. The compressor room stays around 90 to 95 dB.
B: 冲压区最高可达95至105分贝。空压机房基本保持在90至95分贝。
A: What's the national standard limit?
A: 国家标准的限值是多少？
B: The occupational exposure limit is 85 dB for an 8-hour workday.
B: 职业接触限值为8小时工作日85分贝。
A: So we're far above the limit?
A: 那我们很多点位都超标了？
B: Yes, several areas exceed the threshold, which requires urgent action.
B: 是的，多个点位处于噪声超标状态，需要紧急整治。
Part 3: 健康影响（2分钟）
A: What health effects can noise cause?
A: 噪声会对人的健康造成哪些影响？
B: The most direct is noise-induced hearing loss, which is irreversible.
B: 最直接的是噪声性听力损伤，这是不可逆的。
A: Any other effects?
A: 还有其他影响吗？
B: Yes. It can cause tinnitus, high blood pressure, sleep disorders, and even reduced concentration.
B: 有。还会引发耳鸣、高血压、睡眠障碍，甚至注意力下降。
A: Does it affect work safety?
A: 会影响工作安全吗？
B: Absolutely. Workers may miss safety warnings or alarm signals, increasing accident risk.
B: 当然会。工作人员可能听不到安全警告或报警信号，增大事故发生概率。
A: How do we identify early signs?
A: 早期症状怎么识别？
B: Warning signs include muffled hearing after work, needing to raise your voice to be heard, and ringing in the ears.
B: 下班后听力发闷、交谈时需提高嗓音、以及耳内有嗡嗡声，都是早期信号。
Part 4: 管理措施（2分钟）
A: How do we control noise hazards?
A: 噪声危害如何管控？
B: We follow the hierarchy of controls: elimination, substitution, engineering controls, administrative controls, and PPE.
B: 我们遵循分层管控原则：消除、替代、工程控制、管理控制和个体防护。
A: What engineering controls do we use?
A: 我们采用了哪些工程控制手段？
B: We installed soundproof enclosures on punch presses, mufflers on air compressors, and vibration dampers under equipment.
B: 冲压机加装了隔音罩，空压机安装了消音器，设备底部铺设了减振垫。
A: What about administrative controls?
A: 行政管理方面呢？
B: We implement job rotation to limit exposure, post noise warning signs, and conduct annual hearing tests for all exposed workers.
B: 实行岗位轮换减少接触时间，张贴噪声警示标识，同时每年对所有接触岗位人员开展听力检测。
A: What PPE is required?
A: 需要配备哪些个人防护用品？
B: Earplugs or earmuffs with adequate noise reduction rating are mandatory. We also provide custom-molded earplugs for long-term users.
B: 必须佩戴具有足够降噪值的耳塞或耳罩，对长期使用者还提供定制耳塞。
Part 5: ★★★ 完整讨论（2分钟）
A: What's the most challenging part of noise management?
A: 噪声管理中最有挑战性的部分是什么？
B: Getting workers to consistently wear hearing protection. Many feel discomfort and remove it.
B: 最难的是让员工始终如一地佩戴听力防护装备。不少人觉得不舒服就取下来了。
A: How do we address this?
A: 这个问题怎么解决？
B: Through regular training and awareness campaigns. We show workers real cases of hearing loss and its lifelong impact.
B: 通过常态化培训和意识宣贯，让员工直观了解听力损伤的真实案例及其终身影响。
A: How do we monitor effectiveness?
A: 如何监测管控效果？
B: We conduct quarterly noise monitoring in all areas and track hearing test trends year over year.
B: 每季度对所有点位开展噪声监测，并对年度听力检测数据进行趋势分析。
A: Any continuous improvement plans?
A: 有持续改进计划吗？
B: Yes, we plan to upgrade old equipment, add more sound-absorbing panels, and establish quiet zones in the workshop.
B: 有。我们计划淘汰老旧设备、增设吸音板材，并建立车间安静休息区域。
A: Great. Noise management is a long-term commitment.
A: 很好，噪声管理不是短期任务，需要长期坚持。
B: Exactly. With proper controls and worker engagement, we can protect everyone's hearing for a lifetime.
B: 没错，只要管控手段到位、员工自觉配合，就能保障每一位工人的听力不受损害。
练习建议：按顺序练习 Part 1→ Part 2→ Part 3→ Part 4→ Part 5。每部分先慢速练习，再使用正常语速朗读。
